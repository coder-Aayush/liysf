About

The London International Youth Science Forum (LIYSF) is a two week residential event held at Imperial College London where 500 world’s leading young minds from around 70 countries will participate the event. The LIYSF is an event which main motto is to generate and promote new innovative ideas. This two week event will surely inaugurate the student’s point of view which will explore the future developments in the sciences, with lecture demonstrations, specialist seminars and debates led by a team of scientists and experts. This event will provide an opportunities to visit industrial sites, research centers, scientific institutions & organization including world class research institutions and laborites. The majority of participants are supported to attend the forum of prize winners of national and international science competitions. The date of program is from 29 July- 12 August 2020.

Eligibility Criteria for the London International Youth Science Forum.

  • The candidates must have the age 16-21 years.
  • The candidates should write the essay on the given topic by the selector according to their requirement.
  • There will be a group registration after the selection of essay.
  • The program cost is £2095 which is for the non scholarship and for the scholarship holder the program cost is £1695.

Topic for the essay: Role of youth in scientific innovation for the welfare of the society. (1000+ words)

Final Date of Submission: March 20, 2020

Note: The interview will be taken after the selection of the essay submitted.
